### Breadcrumbs SMS phishing campaign

Wyvern Labs is sharing indicators of compromise related to an active SMS phishing campaign targeting Moroccan citizens, tracked internally as Breadcrumbs.

The campaign impersonates the Moroccan Ministry of Justice, luring victims with fraudulent traffic fine notifications and directing them to spoofed domains mimicking amendes.justice.gov.ma for financial credential and OTP harvesting.

Wyvern Labs Threat research team independently identified and collected 83 domains associated with this campaign, including newly registered domains observed within the past 36 hours.
